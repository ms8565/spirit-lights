About

A 2D, mulitplayer platformer game. You will be randomly matched in a two-person level with other users online. Help each other navigate the forest and make it to the shrine.

Use WASD or Arrow keys to move and jump. Hold the space bar to brighten your light. Press R to teleport to the last waypoint.
